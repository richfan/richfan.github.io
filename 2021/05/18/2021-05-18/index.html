To Ariana

发表于 2021-05-18 更新于 2021-09-29 分类于 妞妞原创

NovelsTo ArianaPluvia Fan, May. 12, 2021.

Chapter 1

A extract from Ariana’s email for work:

Respectable Ma’am,

I am a PhD graduate from the university of Cambridge, who studies Novel-writing and Novel-reports. I happened to see your advertisement for a writer, on a street pole. I am contacting you now to apply.

I believe I am suitable for this job because I have numerous awards in writing reports, novels, essays and etc. I also went to the Young Writers’ Summer Camp and was a counselor. I have experience s working with professional reporters and writers. I was awarded the Young Writers’ Cup three years in a row now.

As to my faults, I confess that I have a rather short temper. I do not like to make small talk with people. I am also likely to get nervous before big events or in front of big crowds, so I prefer to stay behind the scenes…

An extract from Ms. Mel’s reply:

Dear Miss Ariana,

We are impressed by your talent. It would be an honour for us to have you join the Global Writers’ Association. Please remember to print your work permit. The work card shall be at the front desk. Please come to No. 22 Creek Street at 8 a.m…

An extract from Catherina’s email:

Dear Ariana,

Congrats on getting the work! The GWA must be really hard to get in to. But I knew you could be get the job with your amazing writing skills! And you can finally come home to the US.

Meanwhile, I have got a job of directing a comedy called ‘Banana Team’. Can you make time and act as the main lead, a blonde girl named Vanessa? ‘Cause you’re the only blonde I know and it would cost way less. Anyway, I will give you a thousand dollars if you accept, not including the money you will get after it comes out.

I’m on vacation in Hawaii now. This place is really paradise. So many exotic flowers and yummy food! The coconuts here are sweet and juicy, the best I ever had…

An extract from Ariana’s journal:

… I think I’m the luckiest person alive to receive these two jobs. But I also have another job; baby-sitting at home. It’s a nice job and the pay is good. I am going to keep doing this for now…
